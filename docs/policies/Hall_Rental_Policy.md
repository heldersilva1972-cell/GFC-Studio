## Rental Details & Pricing

### Function Time
- **Standard rental includes up to 5 hours of function time**
- Additional hours available at **$50 per hour**
- **Important:** Setup and cleanup time are **not included** in your 5 hours of event time

### Pricing Structure

#### Function Hall Rentals
| Renter Type | Rate |
|-------------|------|
| **Members** (Regular, Guest, or Life) | $300 |
| **Non-Members** | $400 |
| **Coalition/Non-Profit Organizations** | $200 (Non-Member) / $100 (Member) |
| **Youth Organizations** | $100 |

*Rates do not include bartender service*

#### Add-On Services
| Service | Fee |
|---------|-----|
| **Bar Service** (includes bartender & alcohol) | $100 |
| **Kitchen Use** (full use for non-members/caterers) | $100 |
| **A/V Equipment** (sound system, microphone, projector) | *Contact for pricing* |
| **Security Deposit** | *Required - refundable* |

## Management of the Hall

### Hall Rental Committee
The management of the hall shall be performed by a **three (3) member committee** consisting of:
- **Vice President** (representing the Board of Directors)
- **Two (2) members** (approved by the membership)

### Committee Responsibilities
The Hall Rental Committee is responsible for:
- ✓ Approving or disapproving all rental requests
- ✓ Reviewing applications and ensuring compliance with club policies
- ✓ Setting attendance limits when necessary
- ✓ Requiring police presence for certain events (cost added to rental fee)
- ✓ Ensuring all fees are paid before approval

### Application Process
1. **Complete Application:** Proper application must be filled out completely
2. **Payment Required:** Hall rental fee must accompany the application to secure the date
3. **Committee Review:** Committee reviews and approves/denies request
4. **Denial Documentation:** If denied, committee documents reasons and submits to Chairman of Board via Vice President

### Member Sponsorship Rules
If a **member is requesting the hall for another person** at the member rate:
- The **member must fill out the application** (not the guest)
- Payment checks (rental fee + security deposit) must be **in the member's name**
- Member assumes full responsibility for the event

## Eligibility Requirements

### Who May Rent the Hall

#### 1. Club Members
Any **Regular, Guest, or Life member** in good standing may rent the hall at member rates.

#### 2. Friends & Family of Members
- Friends or family members of a member in good standing may rent the hall
- **Pricing:** Charged at non-member rates
- **Application Process:** Same as "Management of the Hall" section
- **Note:** Family members eligible to join as Regular members should be encouraged to do so, but this does not affect rental approval—only pricing

#### 3. Outside Organizations
Any outside organization or group may rent the hall at **non-member** or **organization meeting** rates.

## Kitchen Usage Policy

### Members (Regular, Guest, or Life)
- ✓ **Full kitchen use at no additional charge**
- ✓ Must provide all details to Hall Rental Committee
- ✓ Committee must approve plans

### Non-Members & Organizations

#### Light Use (No Additional Fee)
- Heating meatballs, light appetizers
- Preparing pastry trays
- Warming food in pizza ovens

#### Full Use ($100 Fee)
Full kitchen use is permitted **only with committee approval** for:
- Preparing full meals on premises
- Commercial caterer cooking on-site

### Kitchen Rules & Requirements

#### General Rules
- ❌ **NO CLUB UTENSILS** provided (applies to members and non-members)
- ✓ Refrigerator available for storage (remove all perishables after event)
- ✓ Dumpster available for use
- ✓ Coffee pot available by arrangement
- ⚠️ **Lower ovens on gas stove are NOT to be used under any circumstances**
- ✓ Pizza ovens may be used for warming food

#### Caterer Requirements
The Hall Rental Committee shall ensure:
- **Proof of LIABILITY INSURANCE** must be on file with the club
- If caterer has no insurance, **Board of Directors must approve** (via Vice President) before use

## Additional Policies

### Janitorial Services
- Standard cleaning is included in rental fee
- **Additional janitorial effort required:** Cost deducted from Security Deposit

### 50th Anniversary Special
Any Regular, Guest, or Life member celebrating their **50th anniversary** may have the hall at **no charge**:
- Application may be presented by family member or friend
- **Only janitorial fee required**
- If bar service requested, **bartender cost covered by club**

### Advertising Restrictions
When the hall is rented by a group or organization:
- **No outside advertising** unless approved by Hall Rental Committee
- All advertising content must be reviewed and approved in advance

### Policy Changes
Any changes to this procedure must follow this process:

1. **Written Request:** Submit to Chairman of Board of Directors
2. **Board Vote:** Board of Directors votes on the request
3. **Membership Vote:** Board's decision presented to members
4. **Final Decision:** Majority vote of members approves or denies
5. **Implementation:** Change takes effect immediately upon approval
