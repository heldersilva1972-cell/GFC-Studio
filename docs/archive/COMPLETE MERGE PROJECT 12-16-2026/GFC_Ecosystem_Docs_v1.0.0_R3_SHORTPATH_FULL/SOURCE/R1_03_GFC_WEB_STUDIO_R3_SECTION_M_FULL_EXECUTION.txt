M.1 PURPOSE
STATUS: PENDING
This section defines how Studio manages data retention, compliance obligations,
and legal safeguards to ensure content, audit data, and operational records are
handled in a manner that is lawful, traceable, and defensible.

All retention and deletion behavior MUST be intentional, documented, and
auditable. No data loss may occur implicitly.
