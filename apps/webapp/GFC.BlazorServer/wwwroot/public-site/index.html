Who We Are

A Century of Service & Fellowship

The Gloucester Fraternity Club is more than just a building; it's a home for tradition. Since the early 1920s, we have proudly served the Cape Ann area, fostering friendships that last a lifetime.

Whether it's through our charitable work, our lively social gatherings, or simply being a place to gather, the GFC stands as a pillar of the Gloucester community.
